Who we are

The Fish Guy LLC is a small, focused aquarium maintenance company that has been serving the Triangle since 2009. We currently service over a dozen tanks in the area, ranging in size from 10 gallons to 400+ gallons.

Each tank has its own dedicated Fish Guy or Fish Girl who is (safely) obsessed with the well-being and appearance of your aquarium. Our network of local and global suppliers lets us offer competitive prices and reliable access to high-quality equipment, livestock, and supplements.
